As a homeschool mom of 6 children, I have always found it difficult to find activities listed in one easy place. Every year I invariably end up with curriculum that either didn't fit our school style or just didn't turn out the way I wanted. I have yet to find a central location to find things to do, or find and get rid of curriculum that we no longer need. I have undertaken this website to make it easier for homeschoolers to connect and find resources that they need to be the most successful.
